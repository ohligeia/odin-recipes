Sweet scrambled eggs

DESCRIPCIÓN QUE SE VE

What's the most important meal of the day? Pan con manteca, jé.

My favourite moment of the day is breakfast and I often go to sleep thinking about these eggs on buttered toasts.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Eggs (as many as you want, I usually do 1 per toast if I'm super hungry but one medium-large egg should be enough for 2 toasts)
  • Honey
  • Oatmilk (or any milk of preference, this has more to do with consistence than flavour, but keep in mind that oatmilk is also sweet so it's on theme!)

Steps

  1. First, heat your pan. Drizzle some olive oil (please, please use olive oil) and let it heat up over medium heat for a bit.
  2. When it's warm, add a little spoon of honey. Measure with your heart. Once it's melted with the oil, swirl the pan a bit so it covers the surface completely.
  3. Add the egg and a drizzle of oatmilk (not too much, just a tiny little bit it's enough to give it a creamy texture).
  4. Stir constantly until it's done. If you didn't use too much milk, you'll know when the egg is cooked. It shouldn't be runny, just soft.

I like to have my breakfast prepared before so I can immediately take the egg out of the pan so it doesn't cook more. I usually put it on buttered toast with a thin layer of cottage cheese. Then I put some cucumber slices on top, and I sprinkle salt flakes on it. The contrast is amazing.

This should give you enough energy for whatever you do in the mornings, and it you don't need it, well, at least it'll make you happy.
